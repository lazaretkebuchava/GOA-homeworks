Goal Oriented Academy - GOA

What is GOA?

  • Goal-Oriented Academy (GOA) is a prominent educational and training institution based in Tbilisi, Georgia, that focuses on high-demand skills like programming and martial arts.

Core Offerings

  • Programming & IT Training: GOA provides intensive coding courses aimed at preparing students for careers in the tech industry. Their curriculum often includes technical interviews and job placement assistance with partner companies.

  • Martial Arts Academy: The academy also operates a dedicated Martial Arts branch specializing in MMA. It is located at the East European University in Tbilisi and is known for its professional mentoring and
    "move-by-move" teaching approach.

  • Philosophy: The academy markets itself as an "elite environment" designed to help individuals change their lives through motivation and skill-building.
