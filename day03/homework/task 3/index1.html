Mercedes cls

The 2014 Mercedes-Benz CLS 63 AMG is a high-performance luxury sedan powered by a 5.5-liter twin-turbo V8 engine, available in two versions: a standard model with 550 hp and 531 lb-ft of torque, and an S-Model with 577 hp and 590 lb-ft of torque. Both are equipped with a 7-speed automatic transmission and 4MATIC all-wheel drive
