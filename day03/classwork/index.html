iPhone

he iPhone is a line of smartphones developed and marketed by Apple that run iOS, the company's own mobile operating system. The first-generation iPhone was announced by then–Apple CEO and co-founder Steve Jobs on January 9, 2007, at Macworld 2007, and launched later that year. Since then, Apple has annually released new iPhone models and iOS versions; the latest models being the iPhone 17, the higher-end iPhone 17 Pro and 17 Pro Max, and the thinner iPhone Air.

The PlayStation 5 (PS5) is a home video game console with a custom AMD CPU and RDNA 2 GPU, a fast SSD, and a DualSense controller with haptic feedback and adaptive triggers. It supports 4K resolution, 8K video output, and features like 3D audio with the Tempest Engine. You can choose between a version with a disc drive for 4K Blu-ray discs and games, or the digital-only version.
